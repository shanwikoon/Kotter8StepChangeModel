Step 1: Create a Sense of Urgency

TechGrowth Inc. is losing market share to more agile competitors. The company's traditional products are becoming obsolete, but many employees don't see the need for change. As the Change Manager, your first task is to create a sense of urgency.

Kotter's Step 1: Create a Sense of Urgency

Help others see the need for change through a bold, aspirational opportunity statement that communicates the importance of acting immediately. This creates the initial momentum and urgency required to get the change effort started.
